Welcome to Vijayadhith Exports

Our Profile

The foundation of our company “Vijayadhith Exports” was laid with a vision to fulfill the demand of our customers relating to coconuts. Our company established in the year 2012, at vellore and our coconut farm is located at Pollachi, (Tamil Nadu, India).

we are recognized as one of the eminent processor, exporter and supplier of a broad assortment of Semi Husked Coconuts, Export Brown coconuts, Husked Coconuts, Full Husked Coconuts, Edible Copra and Copra - medium in bulk quantities.

We also undertake long-term contracts with various shipping companies. This has allowed us to provide the most reasonable rates while shipping, handling and documenting our products. Our shipment and export dealings are fully transparent, thereby, helping us gain client's trust and satisfaction. We are renowned for delivering our products on time. We work in tandem with agricultural experts and allied professionals in order to take our products to a whole new level.

Made of supreme quality coconut, procured from cultivated farmers. our products are in huge demand amongst our clients. Due to their worthier quality, we have strengthened our hold over international market. We prefer to use supreme quality coconuts . We provide our customers with a facility of customization in terms of packaging so as to make them feel satisfied regarding the shipment of their consignments.

Mr. VP Prakashraja is the Founder of company and with his astute guiding principles we have become able to rapid growth in our organization. We provides our work forces with the requisite guidance necessary for organization to prosper and fructify.

Our employees are efficient enough to meet the specific requirement of the market. Our workers are always ready to go beyond their roles and responsibilities whenever needed. Being good in decision making, our workers always stay ready to learn new techniques and methodologies which are essential for organizational growth. Our team is our greatest strength that has made us shine like start in the industry.

We have been preferred by our customers a number of times relying our industrial expertise. We carry out fair techniques to trade ourselves and always work as per the clients which help us in building trust and faith amongst our clients.

OUR QUALITY

We have gained a reputable position in the field of supplying and exporting a wide array of Coconut Products due to our strict adherence to Total Quality Management Policies. The export quality coconut offered by us has a shelf life guarantee of more than 50 days, the taste. Pollachi coconut is well said loud for its taste and its aroma.
